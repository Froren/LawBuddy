About Us

Our Service

LawBuddy aims to streamline the crime reporting process and get you in touch with the appropriate people quickly. We make the process simple for the user, one text box to write everything that happened. From there, we take care of the rest. The LawBuddy platform analyzes your claim and determines the best set of lawyers/services to assist you. You remain anonymous until both parties consent to work with eachother. We also offer a chatbot to assist you in the process of finding help, should you have trouble finding the right person on your own.

Our Story

We are team of multidisciplinary enthusiasts. We were inspired by the civics hack challenge as a way to give back to the community. After some brainstorming, we decided that one of the best ways to give back to the community was to bolster their voice. By this, we mean to give them a tool to reach the help that they need, no matter the predicament. Our goal is that you spend less trying to find the help you need, and spend more time working towards solving the problem.
